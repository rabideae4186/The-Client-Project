About Us

Founded in 1902, Sunrise Bakery has been baking beautiful desserts for generations. What started as a small neighborhood bakery has grown into a place where tradition, quality, and care come together in every recipe. From flaky pastries to timeless cakes and sweet morning treats, each dessert is made with simple ingredients and a whole lot of love. At Sunrise Bakery, we believe the best days start fresh, right at sunrise.
